About cooking

Chicken Escalope & Vegetable Papillotte - take a chicken cutlet, open it and place it on a piece of aluminum foil - cut into small cubes a tomato, a yellow pepper and a zucchini - put the small cubes of vegetables on the cutlet and close it gently - season to taste - add a sprig of thyme - close to form a papillotte - bake 15-20 mins, watch for cooking
